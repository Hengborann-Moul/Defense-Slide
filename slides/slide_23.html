Engagement is the only state where composite features beat blendshapes at the top of the SHAP ranking. eye_activity_max carries a SHAP value of 0.19, the single highest value across all four states. This validates the feature engineering pipeline: raw MediaPipe output alone would have missed this signal. eye_activity_p75 reinforces the eye-activity story, while corr_boredom_related_jaw and corr_composite_mouth show that cross-group correlation features matter. The boredom_indicator_std composite appears with negative SHAP, pushing predictions away from engagement when boredom-like jaw dynamics are present. Theoretically, this means engagement is not about facial muscle activation per se, it is about coordinated, active visual exploration.
