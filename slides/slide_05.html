Reading the Learner Through Four Affective States

Boredom, Engagement, Confusion, and Frustration are the four canonical indicators of learning effectiveness — each calls for a different pedagogical intervention.

Why this matters for adaptive learning systems
Real-time or near real-time detection of these four states enables just-in-time pedagogical support — slowing down, offering hints, or adjusting difficulty before the learner disengages completely.
