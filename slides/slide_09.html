RESEARCH OBJECTIVES

Three Concrete Objectives That Anchor the Thesis

Each objective maps to a methodological contribution in Chapter 3 and an empirical finding in Chapter 4, together they form the spine of this defense.

01
Design a comprehensive facial feature extraction pipeline that captures geometric, statistical, and temporal characteristics from the DAiSEE dataset, including eye aspect ratios, head pose angles, mouth opening degrees, eyebrow positions, and their temporal derivatives, as input features for attention level classification.

A short note on this slide: the original thesis-defense template did not include an explicit objectives slide, and I added it deliberately so that the spine of the work is visible to the committee up front. The three objectives are sequential. Objective one is to design a comprehensive facial feature extraction pipeline that captures geometric, statistical, and temporal characteristics from the DAiSEE dataset, eye aspect ratios, head pose angles, mouth opening degrees, eyebrow positions, and their temporal derivatives. Objective two builds on that pipeline and develops an X-G-Boost classifier tuned with regularization for the three-level engagement annotation schema of DAiSEE. Objective three takes the trained model and provides an interpretable analysis using X-G-Boost native feature importance and S-H-A-P values. The mapping card at the bottom shows exactly where each objective is delivered later in the deck.
