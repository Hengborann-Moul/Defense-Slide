The pipeline is twelve stages. We start with raw DAiSEE video at stage one, collapse the four intensity labels to three actionable classes at stage two, extract 78 dimensions per frame with MediaPipe at stage three, build a uniform 150-by-78 matrix at stage four, normalize with five type-specific strategies at stage five, and engineer roughly 2,152 features in five categories at stage six. Stages three, four and five are the topic of slides 14 and 15, and stage six is slide 16. Stage seven then selects the top 500 features via XGBoost plus LASSO; stages nine, ten and eleven — SMOTE, XGBoost training, and isotonic calibration — are slide 18. The pipeline is fully modular: each stage can be swapped independently without rewriting the others.
