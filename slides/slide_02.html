This slide outlines the four sections of today's defense. We will begin with the Introduction, where I will cover the motivation, prior work on the DAiSEE benchmark, our explicit research objectives, and the three research questions this thesis answers. The Methodology section that follows will be the most detailed part of the talk, walking through the DAiSEE dataset, the MediaPipe feature extraction pipeline, our hand-crafted feature engineering, and the XGBoost model design. After that, Results and Discussion will present numerical performance, per-class breakdown, SHAP-based interpretability. Finally, the Conclusion will cover limitations, contributions, and future work.
