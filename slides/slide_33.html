These eight references anchor the thesis. The full bibliography is in the thesis document. Reference 2, the DAiSEE paper by Gupta and colleagues, is the dataset we built on. References 4 by D'Mello and Graesser provides the affective-transition theory that explains our cross-state SHAP findings. Reference 5 by Chen and Guestrin is the XGBoost paper, and reference 6 by Lundberg and Lee is the SHAP paper that grounds our interpretability analysis. Reference 8 by Takefuji is a recent critique of feature-importance methods that contextualizes our use of permutation importance alongside SHAP. I am happy to discuss any of them in Q&A.
