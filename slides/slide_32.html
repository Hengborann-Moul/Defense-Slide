Five concrete future directions, each tied to a specific limitation. Direction one, multimodal fusion, is the most impactful for Confusion, the weakest state, because interaction logs, audio prosody, and physiology carry signal the webcam cannot see. Direction two, real-time deployment optimization, is what brings the work to actual classrooms through model distillation and browser-based deployment. Direction three, cross-dataset evaluation on EngageWild, EMI-Cap, and a new demographically balanced dataset, is what would unlock generalization claims. Direction four, temporal modeling with change-point detection and HMM features, captures sequential patterns lost in statistical aggregation. Direction five, personalized few-shot calibration, accounts for individual differences in facial expressivity. The work aligns with UN SDGs 4, 9, and 10.
