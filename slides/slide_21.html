WHY FRUSTRATION IS EASIEST (ACC = 0.786)

Frustration produces distinctive muscular tension patterns — pressed lips, clenched jaw, brow furrow. These blendshape signatures survive the statistical aggregation step cleanly and give XGBoost strong separability. CAVEAT: 0.786 accuracy is inflated by the Low-class dominance — Frustration Medium/High recall collapses to 0.

WHY CONFUSION IS HARDEST (F1 = 0.566)

Confusion distributes its discriminative signal across many weak features (see Slide 24). The model defaults to predicting Low for 98% of confusion samples — recall on Medium and High drops to 0.040 and 0.012.

Confusion-matrix pattern: Boredom spreads evenly across adjacent classes; Engagement collapses Low→Medium; Confusion and Frustration collapse everything to Low.
